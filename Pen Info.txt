Title:

3D CSS Paper Shredder

-------------------------------------------------------------------

Description:

This pen combines the power of 3D CSS, background-clip, and a touch of JS to create a functional 3D paper shredder. Type to edit the text on the paper then press the green button to shred your text!

-------------------------------------------------------------------

Tags:

css, 3d, shredder, background-clip, gradients

Wanted to challenge myself to make something interesting this week so combined the power of 3D CSS and a touch of JS to create a functional 3D paper shredder! Type to edit the text on the paper then press the green button to shred your text! @CodePen https://codepen.io/GeorgePark/full/QWrxPyJ [video]

The shredded text effect is achieved by with a repeating linear gradient on both the paper and text add then applying 'background-clip: text' [screenshot(s)]
